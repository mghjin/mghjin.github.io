Current Project Integrate

Northeastern University GSND 6320 (Pscyhology of Play) Research Project

Game Engine: Unity (2D)

Duration: Ongoing

Jin is the Lead Narrative Designer, Assistant Programmer & Level Designer, and Sound Designer for this school game research project. The goal of the project is to test the psychological concept of "conformity" and "obedience" within the cyberpunk environment and narrative. As the player progresses through the levels, their decisions will impact gameplay in subsequent levels, as well as the player's personal ending.

Digital Game Project Imugi/Dragon

Northeastern University GSND 5110 (Game Design and Analysis) Digital Game Project

Game Engine: Unity (2D)

Duration: 7 weeks

As the Lead Story/Concept Designer as well as the Character Artist/Animator, Jin helped the other members of the team to understand the specific goal mechanics and aesthetics of the game. He was also the Assistant Programmer. The game was inspired by Korean folklore about "Imugi", also known as lesser dragons. The narrative follows the Imugi character in its descending journey to find strenght, wisdom, and empathy before then becoming a true Dragon.

Fiction Comic Project "Plum Rain" in WANDERLUST

Jin worked together with a professional illustrator in the ambitious WANDERLUST project, successfully Kickstarted at the end of 2017. As the entire anthology focused on the themes "Quests and Journeys", the story "Plum Rain" tells of the ancient Sillan warriors of South Korea, the Hwarang and of otherworldly spirits commonplace in Korean mythology. As the story writer of the comic, Jin was responsible for delivering narrative and scripting the panels in the comic accordingly.
