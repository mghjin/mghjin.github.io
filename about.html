Game Designer & Explorer

Outside of studies, Jin Hae Kim enjoys learning languages, visiting new countries, and practicing Taekwondo (and recently Wing Chun style Kung Fu). He believes that experiences both inside and outside one's cultural knowledge help individual growth, and that they hold potential for interesting stories.

Throughout the years, he has grown to have a deep fascination for East Asian mythology and folklore, particularly within Korea and Japan. He strives to bring his understandings about cultures and technology together within his work.

His passion for writing and learning more about his Korean heritage has led to collaborating with an artist to publish a comic in WANDERLUST, A Mythology Anthology. Jin is also an active member of MIT's Oori Samulnori group! Given time and space, he practices Bassoon, and hopes to be part of a professional orchestra in the future.
